## The Problem

The flooring and construction industry is fragmented and underserved by modern sales technology. Contractors — including epoxy specialists, polished concrete companies, and industrial floor coating businesses — are difficult to find, qualify, and reach at scale.

**Manual lead generation is broken:**
- Sales teams spend 60%+ of their time searching for contacts
- Business directories have 40–60% stale or incomplete data
- No scoring system exists to prioritize high-value leads
- Outreach is inconsistent and untracked

**The result:** thousands of qualified contractor relationships are never initiated.

---

## The Solution

**XPS Lead Intelligence Platform** is a fully autonomous B2B lead generation system built specifically for the flooring and construction industries.

The platform:
- 🔍 **Discovers** contractors across Google Maps, Bing, Yelp, and directories
- ✅ **Validates** contact data (phone, website, email reachability)
- 📊 **Enriches** records with emails, LinkedIn profiles, and additional contacts
- 🏆 **Scores** leads 0–100 using a proprietary model (completeness + quality + industry)
- 📧 **Automates outreach** with personalized email sequences
- 📱 **Displays leads** in a real-time PWA dashboard
- 🤖 **Operates autonomously** via GitHub Actions — no human required
