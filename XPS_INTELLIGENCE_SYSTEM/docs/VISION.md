## Mission

Build a fully autonomous contractor lead intelligence platform that discovers, enriches, scores, and converts high-value flooring and construction contractors into paying customers — operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with zero manual intervention.

---

## The Problem

Small and mid-size contractors in the epoxy, polished concrete, flooring, and industrial construction industries are underserved by modern B2B outreach. Existing solutions require:

- **Manual research** — hours spent finding contact info
- **Low data quality** — incomplete phone/email/address records
- **No scoring** — no way to prioritize which leads are worth pursuing
- **Disconnected tools** — CRM, scraper, email, analytics all separate

The result: sales teams waste time on cold, incomplete, or duplicate leads.

---

## Our Solution

The XPS Lead Intelligence Platform automates the entire lead lifecycle:

| Stage | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Discovery** | Scrapes Google Maps, Bing Maps, Yelp, and contractor directories |
| **Validation** | Deduplicates, verifies phone/email/website |
| **Enrichment** | Discovers emails, LinkedIn profiles, additional contacts |
| **Scoring** | Ranks leads 0–100 based on completeness, quality, and industry signals |
| **Outreach** | Sends personalized email campaigns with automated follow-ups |
| **Dashboard** | Real-time PWA dashboard to view, filter, and act on leads |
| **Autonomy** | GitHub Actions run the full pipeline on schedule, no human required |

---

## Core Principles

1. **Autonomy First** — Every component should work unattended via scheduled GitHub Actions.
2. **Data Quality Over Quantity** — 100 enriched, scored leads beat 10,000 raw records.
3. **Open Source by Design** — No proprietary lock-in; all logic is inspectable and extensible.
4. **Modular Architecture** — Every stage is a standalone agent that can be tested, replaced, or scaled independently.
5. **Governance Embedded** — Privacy, security, and compliance are not afterthoughts.
